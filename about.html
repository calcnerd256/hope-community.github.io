Community Mission

As disciples of Jesus Christ, we seek to glorify God by…

  1. Proclaiming the gospel to un-reached people in our community and beyond (Matthew 28:18-20, Romans 15:20-21, Acts 20:24, 2 Corinthians 5:14-21, Romans 1:16)
  2. Raising up mature, reproducing disciples of Jesus Christ (2 Timothy 2:2, Matthew 28:18-20, Matthew 13:23, Colossians 1:28-29, Ephesians 4:11-16)
  3. Working to bring God’s heart of mercy, compassion, love, justice, and reconciliation to our community (Matthew 25:31-46, Luke 6:27-36, James 1:27, 1 John 3:14-17, Isaiah 58, Amos 5, Colossians 3:11, Galatians 3:28-29)
  4. Intentionally training and sending out teams to work towards these goals in other communities across the world (Romans 10:13-15, Acts 13:2-4, Matthew 28:18-20, Romans 15:20-21, Matthew 24:14, Revelation 5:9-10)
