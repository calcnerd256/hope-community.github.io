Gospel Project

The Gospel Project is a two month discipleship training and missions immersion experience in Oklahoma City and Norman for students and adults sponsored by Hope Community Church and Christ Community Church. The Gospel Project will be June 1 through July 31. Gospel Project participants will:

  • Live in an intimate community of people seeking to know Christ
  • Share the message of Jesus and help plant house churches in an inner-city context
  • Participate in mercy and justice ministries that seek to tangibly demonstrate the love of Christ to the world
  • Receive discipleship and urban ministry training from a variety of established leaders
  • Devote significant time to personal spiritual formation through Bible study, prayer, and a lifestyle of worship in community
